Rules

Poll opens at 12:00 AM IST on the game day and locks at 6:00 PM IST. Users can edit their prediction until 5:59 PM IST.
  • Prediction options: White win, Draw, Black win.
  • Leaderboards are separated for Open and Women’s tournaments.
  • Leaderboard accuracy uses only finished games that the user predicted in that tournament.
  • Missed games do not count as wrong picks.
  • Round-wise predictions become visible only after that round is complete.
  • Only the admin can enter actual results.
